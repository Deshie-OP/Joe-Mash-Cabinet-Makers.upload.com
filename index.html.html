Welcome to our site

Our company specializes in Kitchen Units, Build-In Wardrobes and Floating TV Stands. We are professional cabinet makers as well as a registered business under the government from 2016. Our work includes in planning a full diagram of everything, assembling all our tools and woods, building the carboards as we can work anywhere from kitchens, bedrooms, and living rooms. Our carboards always includes the best of our efforts and exercising the full extent of our capabilities. We always do the best to deliver the best results from ourselves.

About Us

We buy woods from different companies based in Silverton but mostly we buy by Cut N Edge as we got the best services and woods from them. Our first course of action is taking pictures before build-in and after build-in. Starting with assembling, moving on to the placement of the carboard size by size where it belongs, only once assembling is completed. We than start placing them on the walls, starting from the bottom carboard first, then moving up with the top carboards, and therefore measuring first and correctly is extremely important to us. We always check the quality of the woods first before assembling as we want to give the best results possible.
